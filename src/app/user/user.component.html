Welcome to the Customer Overview page of my CRM system!

This page serves as the central hub for efficiently managing your contacts. Here's a brief guide on how to navigate and use this page:

Adding Contacts:
To add contacts, start by clicking the "Add Contact" button located in the top right corner of the page. Enter the necessary information and click "Save" to add the contact to your list.
Viewing Contacts:
A list of your contacts will appear in the main section of the page. Here you will find important information such as names and important details, which can also be changed.
Editing Contacts:
If you need to update information for a specific contact, simply click on their name in the list. This will take you to an editable view of their details, where you can make any necessary changes, corrections, or updates as needed.
