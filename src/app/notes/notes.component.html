Welcome to the Notes Page of my CRM System!

Welcome to the Notes feature of our CRM system! This tool makes jotting down and managing notes a breeze. Here's a brief guide on how to utilize it:

Adding Notes:
Select the "+ Add Note" button to create your note. This note can be any crucial information or reminder. Once you've finished composing your note, click the "Save" button. Your note will then be visible on the page.
Viewing Notes:
You'll find a list of your added notes on the page, with the most recent notes located at the bottom.
Deleting Notes:
To remove a note, click the "Delete Note" button in the note you want to delete.
