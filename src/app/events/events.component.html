Welcome to the Events Overview page of my CRM system!

This page acts as a central hub for effectively managing your events. Here's a straightforward guide on how to navigate and make the most of this page:

Adding Events:
To begin, click the "Add Event" button situated in the bottom right corner of the page. Complete the required event details, such as the event name, date, location, and any other pertinent information. After you've finished, press "Save" to include the event in your list.
Viewing Events:
The main section of the page showcases a list of all your events, where you can access vital information such as event names and key details. Simply scroll through this list for a swift overview of your events.
Detailed Event Information:
Upon clicking on an event, you'll gain access to more comprehensive information about that specific event, including details about attendees, the schedule, and any related data. Additionally, you'll have the option to delete the event if needed to maintain an updated event list.
